HTML NOTES
Header Tag
Creates a "Header", putting emphasis on whatever is written between the two tags, by placing it at the top of the page.
P Tag
Allows people to create a paragraph that can contain whatever they want.
em Tag
Used to indicate when a certain amount of text is more usefull of important than others, this will also be illustrated when a screen reader passes over the text.
Nav Tag
Allows the coder to create a place where all the anchors for the other pages of the site to be located.
Title Tag
Names the page of whatever it is coded on.
